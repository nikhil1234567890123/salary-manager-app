# Future Roadmap: Salary Manager

The following features and improvements are planned for future releases of the Salary Manager app.

## Phase 1: Enhanced Intelligence
- **AI Categorization**: Automatically move merchants into categories (Food, Travel, etc.) using LLM-based classification.
- **Financial Health Score**: A gamified score based on how well the user sticks to their "Safe Daily Spend."

## Phase 2: Synchronization & Connectivity
- **Multi-device Sync**: Implement Supabase real-time sync for cross-device access.
- **Bank Account Linking**: Direct integration with Account Aggregators (AA) for real-time bank feeds.

## Phase 3: Financial Management
- **Subscription Tracking**: Identify recurring payments and notify users before renewal.
- **Bill Reminders**: Automated alerts for Rent, Electricity, and Credit Card bills.

## Phase 4: Reports & Planning
- **Detailed PDF Reports**: Generate visual monthly PDF summaries with charts.
- **Investment Suggestions**: Nudge users to invest their surplus "Safe Spend" into low-risk funds.
